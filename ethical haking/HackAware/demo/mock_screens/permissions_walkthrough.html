App Permissions Walkthrough

Step 1: Open App Settings

Navigate to your device's settings and find the 'Apps' section.

Step 2: Select the App

Choose the app for which you want to review permissions.

Step 3: Review Permissions

Check the permissions requested by the app. Ensure they are necessary for the app's functionality.

Step 4: Adjust Permissions

If you find unnecessary permissions, toggle them off to enhance your privacy.

Step 5: Confirm Changes

Make sure to confirm any changes you make to the app's permissions.
