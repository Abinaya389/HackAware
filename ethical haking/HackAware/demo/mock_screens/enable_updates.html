How to Enable Updates

Step 1: Open Settings

Go to your device's settings by tapping on the settings icon.

Step 2: Navigate to System

Scroll down and select "System" from the list of options.

Step 3: Software Update

Tap on "Software Update" to check for available updates.

Step 4: Enable Auto-Updates

Make sure to enable the option for automatic updates to keep your device secure.

Step 5: Check Update History

You can also view your update history to see what has been installed.
